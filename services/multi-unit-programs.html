  • Priority support for re-inspections and clearance exams
  • Buildings of any size—we handle 50+ unit properties with ease

  • Owners of duplexes, triplexes, and 4-plexes

Reporting That Scales

Most assessors avoid large buildings because the reporting burden is overwhelming. We built systems that deliver the same photo-documented, component-level detail for every unit—whether it's a duplex or a 50-unit apartment building. Each unit gets its own complete report, and you get a consolidated summary for your records and city submission. The result: faster turnaround, better documentation, and reports the City of Cleveland recognizes as best-in-class.

FAQs

Do you handle tenant communication?
We coordinate with property managers but do not directly manage tenant notifications unless arranged.
Can I combine services for a discount?
Yes. Combining risk assessments, dust wipes, and clearance exams for multiple units lowers cost and reduces disruption.
How do I track results for multiple units?
We provide consolidated portfolio reports plus unit-by-unit documentation for compliance records.
