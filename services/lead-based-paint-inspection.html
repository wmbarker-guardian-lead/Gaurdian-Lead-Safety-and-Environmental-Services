Lead-Based Paint Inspection

Room-by-room testing via XRF or paint-chip sampling to locate lead-based paint and plan compliance. Pass your inspection and qualify for Cleveland’s 20-Year Lead-Safe Exemption.

Book an Inspection

What’s Included

XRF or Lab Chip Analysis

Every painted surface tested room-by-room using portable XRF technology or lab-analyzed paint chips. Complete component mapping tells you exactly where lead-based paint exists.

Clear Location Report

Detailed report identifying every surface tested, results by component, and a clear summary of which areas contain lead-based paint and which are clear.

Compliance Planning

Results formatted to support your next steps—whether that’s abatement planning, renovation scoping, daycare licensing, or pursuing the 20-Year Exemption.

20-Year Lead-Safe Exemption

When your property passes a lead-based paint inspection with no hazards found—or after all identified hazards are properly abated—you may qualify for Cleveland’s 20-Year Lead-Safe Exemption. That’s two decades of compliance without repeated inspections.

The Pathway

  • Lead-based paint inspection (XRF or chip)
  • Abatement scope & licensed contractor (if needed)
  • Clearance exam & LIRA documentation
  • 20-Year Exemption filing

Funding May Be Available

The Lead Safe Home Fund and other programs can offset abatement costs. We help you navigate eligibility and applications.
