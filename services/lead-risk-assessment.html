Lead Risk Assessment

We inspect painted surfaces, take dust wipe samples, and, if applicable, paint-chip and soil samples around the home or facility. You’ll get a clear, actionable report with next steps for compliance.

What’s Included

  • Interview & visual assessment of all painted surfaces
  • Dust wipes (floors, window sills, troughs)
  • Paint-chip / soil sampling (if applicable)
  • Accredited lab analysis & interpretation
  • Photo-documented, component-level report
  • Prioritized remediation guidance
  • City-submission-ready documentation

Who It’s For

  • Rental properties across Greater Cleveland & nearby suburbs
  • Owner-occupied homes built before 1978
  • Child care & child-occupied facilities

Turnaround

Typical 10–14 days (rush options available).
