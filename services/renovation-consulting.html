FAQs

Do I need a certified renovator for every project?
Yes. EPA requires at least one certified renovator to oversise lead-safe work practices on each covered job site.
Can you train my crew?
We do not provide RRP certification courses directly, but we connect you with accredited trainers and help prepare your team.
Do you assist with documentation?
Yes. We guide you in maintaining proper records, checklists, and final reports to meet EPA and local requirements.
