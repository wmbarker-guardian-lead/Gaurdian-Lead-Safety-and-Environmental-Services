Child-Care & Schools

Protecting the spaces where little ones learn, play, and grow.

Serving preschools, daycares, Head Start programs, and K-6 schools across Northeast Ohio.

We help child-care providers and schools meet lead safety compliance with care, thoroughness, and minimal disruption to your daily routine.

Why Lead Safety Matters Here

Children under 6 are the most vulnerable to lead exposure. Facilities where they spend significant time have specific compliance obligations.

📜

Regulatory Requirements

Facilities where children under 6 spend 6 or more hours per week must meet HUD and Ohio Department of Health lead safety standards.

🔍

Clearance Before Occupancy

Clearance exams must be completed before children can safely occupy a newly renovated or remediated space.

📅

Ongoing Certification

Lead-safe certification requires regular renewal. We help you stay ahead of deadlines so your facility remains in good standing.

Daycare Focus Areas

Licensed daycares receive targeted assessments aligned with ODH and local rules, prioritizing hazard control where children are most at risk.

✋

High-Contact Surfaces

Window sills, door frames, railings, and any painted surface within a child's reach are tested and documented.

🌳

Exterior Soil & Play Areas

Outdoor play zones and bare soil near the building are sampled for lead contamination.

🛌

Entry & Nap Areas

Entryways where dust tracks in and nap rooms where children rest on the floor get special attention.

What We Provide

  • Comprehensive risk assessments tailored for child-occupied facilities
  • XRF testing and dust wipe sampling of all accessible surfaces
  • Detailed reports with clear findings and recommendations
  • Reports matched to ODH compliance forms for easy licensing
  • Guidance on interim controls and hazard reduction strategies
  • Full documentation support for ODH licensing and compliance
