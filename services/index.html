Our Services

Licensed inspections, testing, and consulting for Cleveland homes, rentals, and child-care facilities.

Lead Risk Assessment

Comprehensive investigation of painted surfaces, dust wipe sampling, and soil testing. Clear, actionable reports with next steps for Cleveland Lead-Safe Certification.

Lead-Based Paint Inspection

Room-by-room XRF and paint-chip testing to locate lead-based paint. Qualify for Cleveland's 20-Year Lead-Safe Exemption and eliminate re-certification requirements.

Lead Clearance Exam

Post-renovation and abatement verification. HUD/EPA-compliant dust wipe sampling to confirm your property is safe for re-occupancy.

Renovation Consulting (RRP)

Expert guidance for contractors and property owners working under EPA Renovation, Repair, and Painting rules. Stay compliant throughout your project.

Multi-Family & Multi-Unit

From duplexes to large apartment buildings—volume pricing, efficient scheduling, and the most detailed reporting in the market.

Child-Care & Schools

Specialized assessments for daycares, preschools, and K-6 schools. Aligned with ODH licensing requirements and designed for minimal disruption.
